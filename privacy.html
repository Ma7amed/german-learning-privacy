Privacy Policy

App: Deutsch Cards

Developer: Brewed Labs

Last updated: 2026-01-26

1) Overview

Deutsch Cards helps users learn German vocabulary using collections and cards. The app works offline by default. Optional cloud backup is available.

2) Data the App Stores

  • Local data: vocabulary words, translations, user-created collections, and optional images. Images may be selected by the user from their device and are stored locally on the device.

3) Google User Data (Optional Google Drive Backup)

If you enable Google Drive backup, the app accesses and stores data only in your Google Drive App Data (an app-private area). This may include vocabulary data and user-selected images used in cards.

  • The app reads/writes only its own backup data in the Google Drive App Data folder.
  • The app does not browse, read, or modify your other Google Drive files.

The developer cannot view, access, or retrieve your Google Drive backups.

4) How We Use Data

Data is used only to provide the app’s functionality, including:

  • Saving and restoring your vocabulary collections
  • Syncing backups to your own Google Drive (only when you choose to enable it)
  • Storing user-selected images locally and restoring them from the user’s own Google Drive backup

We do not use Google user data for advertising, analytics, or marketing.

5) Data Sharing

Deutsch Cards does not sell or share your data with third parties.

6) Data Storage & Security

  • Local data remains on your device.
  • If Google Drive backup is enabled, vocabulary data and user-selected images are stored in your Google Drive App Data.
  • Brewed Labs does not store your backups on its own servers.

The developer cannot view, access, or retrieve your Google Drive backups.

7) User Control & Deletion

  • You can use the app offline without Google Drive.
  • You can disable Google Drive access at any time from the app or from your Google Account permissions.
  • You can delete app backups from Google Drive App Data and/or uninstall the app to remove local data.
